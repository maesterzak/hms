What is a Hospital Management System?

This project Online Hospital Management System aims at to develop the software that covers all the aspects of management and operations of hospital. It enables healthcare providers to improve operational effectiveness, reduce costs, reduce medical errors, reduce time consumption and enhance delivery of quality of care.
